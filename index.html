About Our Kindergarten

At Little Bloom, we create a nurturing environment where children aged 2-6 can explore, discover, and develop through play-based learning. Our experienced educators foster each child's natural curiosity in a warm, welcoming setting.

Playful Learning

Engaging activities that make learning fun and memorable for young minds.

Safe Environment

Child-friendly spaces designed with safety as our top priority.

Expert Teachers

Certified educators specializing in early childhood development.

Nurturing Care

Individual attention to support each child's unique needs.

Our Programs

Playhouse

For ages 2-3: Sensory play and social development in a safe, stimulating environment with caring staff.

Nursery

For ages 3-4: Early literacy and numeracy through play, music, and creative activities.

LKG

For ages 4-5: Structured learning with fun activities to develop cognitive and motor skills.

UKG

For ages 5-6: School readiness program focusing on language, math, and social skills.

Special Activities & Enrichment

Beyond academics, we offer diverse activities that help children discover their interests and talents.

Creative Arts

Drawing, painting, and crafts to enhance creativity and fine motor skills

Music & Dance

Classical and folk music, rhythm activities, and traditional dance forms

Storytelling

Interactive stories, role-play, and puppet shows for language development

Math Concepts

Number games, pattern recognition, and hands-on mathematical exploration
